Neuse River monitoring site associated with Milburnie Dam removal project. Site is upstream of the dam, top of impounded reach.  Onset DO and water level loggers are attached to Buffaloe Rd bridge. 
This site is maintained by the Doyle lab, Duke University.





--- Data status codes ---

Data level codes:
	R: Raw (directly from sensor/logger)
	O: Outliers/anomalies removed
	G: Gaps imputed
	C: Corrected for drift (due to biofouling, calibration, etc.)
	D: Derived from other variables
	-: No information
